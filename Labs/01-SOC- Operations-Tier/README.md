# Lab 01 - SOC Operations & Tier-1 Responsibilities

## Objective

The objective of this lab was to understand how a Security Operations Center (SOC) functions, the responsibilities of Tier-1, Tier-2, and Tier-3 analysts, and how security alerts move through the incident response lifecycle from detection to closure.

## Tasks Completed

- Studied the roles and responsibilities of Tier-1, Tier-2, and Tier-3 SOC analysts.
- Learned the alert lifecycle from detection to incident closure.
- Created a SOC alert workflow diagram showing the path from detection, triage, escalation, and closure.
- Documented the responsibilities of each SOC tier in a summary file.

## Deliverables

- `SOC_TierFlow.png`
- `TierRoles.txt`

## What I Learned

- The responsibilities of each SOC analyst tier.
- How security alerts are triaged before escalation.
- The importance of documentation during incident response.
- How SOC teams work together to detect, investigate, and respond to threats.
