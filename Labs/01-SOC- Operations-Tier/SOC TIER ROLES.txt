SOC TIER ROLES
Tier 1(Security Monitoring and alert Triage)
Responsibilities:
- Monitor SIEM Dashboards
- Review security alerts
- Validate alerts
- Perform Initial validation
- Collect evidence
- Escalate confirmed Incidents to Tier 2
- Document findings

Tier 2 (Incident Investigation and Response)
Responsibilities:
- Perform deeper forensic investigations
- Analyze malware and suspicious activity
- Contain and remediate incidents
- Coordinate with IT teams
- Support Tier 1 analysts

Tier 3 (Threat Hunting and Detection Engineering)
Responsibilities:
- Hunt for advanced threats
- Develop detection rules
- Improve SOC monitoring capabilities
- Analyze attacker techniques using MITRE ATT&CK
- Tune SIEM and EDR detections
